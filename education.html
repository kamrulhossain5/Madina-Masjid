Madina Youth
Madina Youth

Madina Youth is dedicated to serving the East Village and Lower East Side community through education and youth empowerment. We focus on developing leadership and unity within the local youth by instilling values, teamwork, and respect. Providing services and opportunities to serve God is at the forefront of Madina Youth. We work hand in hand with other local organizations, charities, schools, universities, and inter-faith programs to better our community. We also give back to the neighborhood by educating people about Islam through Da’wah and reviving the traditions of the final Prophet Mohammed (pbuh). In a time of disconnection from Allah, Madina Youth’s objective is to also link the future leaders of tomorrow with God by better serving humanity in the way we were taught by our beloved Messenger.

Kids' Education Program
Children Weekend & Summer Education Program

Madina Masjid provides a comprehensive weekday and weekend education system for our children. The weekend classes take place on Saturday and Sunday from 10:00 am to 1:30 pm and the Summer Program will start July 7 to August 31. Students will attend Saturday - Wednesday from 10:30 am to 1:30 pm.

The classes are focus on:
• Reciting Quran with Tajweed
• Fiqh of Salah
• Fiqh of Sawm
• Fiqh of Food and Drinks
• Manners and Characters
• Islamic History
• Becoming a Better Muslim in America
• Calling Towards Allah

Adult Classes
Adult Classes

Madina Masjid currently provides several classes to adults. The masjid will continue to grow the adult classes platform and continue to provide various course for adults focused on Islam and the Quran.

• Tajweed and Tafsir Class
• Fiqh Class with Imam
• Reflections Halaqa and A Journey Through the Verses of the Quran
