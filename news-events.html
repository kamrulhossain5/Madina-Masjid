News & Events

Islamic Council of America, Inc.
Ramadan
Join us every night in Ramadan for a community Iftar dinner to break your fast. Madina Masjid is an inclusive space that caters toward Muslims from all theological backgrounds, sects, ideologies and practices. We embrace our diversity and believe a strong community should be built upon this. This Ramadan, come experience what community should feel like. During Ramadan, our iftar dinners serve over 300 people a day. After Isha prayer, we will also have Taraweeh prayers led by two Imams (10PM for Khatem Taraweeh and Regular Taraweeh at 1PM). Finally, at the end of the month, we will celebrate Eid al-Fitr with prayers and brunch open to the entire community! These are just some of the numerous services offered here at Madina Majid.
Eid 2019
Madina Masjid will be hosting Eid services on Saturday, August 10th to honor the commemoration of Ibrahim’s willingness to sacrifice his son in obedience of a command from Allah and to mark the end of the annual Hajj to Mecca inshallah.

On Saturday, August 10th, tentative to local moon sighting, we will celebrate Eid ul Adha in Open Road Lower East Side Playground with a prayer service lead by Imam Yusuf Al-Majid followed by an outdoor community meet and greet.
