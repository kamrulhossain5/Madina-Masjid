About Us

Islamic Council of America, Inc.

History

Madina Masjid (also known as, Islamic Council of America) is a non-profit, religious and cultural organization, established in 1976. It is the second oldest masjid in New York City.

A diversity of travelers, taxi drivers, professionals, scholars, local musallies, youth, and new Muslims from the expanses of multi-lingual cosmopolitan New York utilize this Islamic center. It is a global microcosm of the Muslim world bursting at the seams.

We have been working diligently to renovate the property for more extended permanent activities and services.
